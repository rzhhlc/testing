About the Puzzles

Welcome to this collection of procedural puzzles. Each game found on the dashboard is designed to provide infinite variety through algorithmic generation, ensuring that no two sessions are ever exactly the same.

The philosophy behind these challenges is simple: logic and arithmetic should be accessible, tactile, and rewarding. From the geometric intricacies of Fields and Fences to the mathematical rigor of Setting the Table, each puzzle encourages the player to discover their own unique strategies.

Whether you are looking for a quick five-minute diversion or a deep dive into complex logic with Sudoku or Summit, there is something here for every curious mind.

The final entry, Say What?, shifts the focus from numbers to letters, drawing from a vast database of historical aphorisms to challenge your linguistic intuition.

Thank you for playing. Have fun!
